# AI Search Research

Open research exploring how AI search engines discover, evaluate, trust, and cite websites, brands, and content.

This repository documents practical experiments, datasets, observations, and frameworks focused on understanding modern AI search systems, including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

The goal is to build a public knowledge base that helps founders, marketers, researchers, and developers better understand AI visibility, LLM citations, trust signals, and the evolving search ecosystem.


## Research Areas

- AI Visibility
- LLM Citation Analysis
- AI Trust Signals
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- AI Overviews
- Entity & Semantic SEO
- Reddit & Community Signals
- Brand Authority
- Content Quality
- Technical SEO for AI Search
- Prompt Engineering
- Search Automation

## Research Methodology

Every experiment follows a structured process to ensure findings are repeatable and easy to validate.

1. Define the research question.
2. Develop a hypothesis.
3. Test across multiple AI search platforms.
4. Document observations and evidence.
5. Compare results.
6. Publish findings and datasets.
